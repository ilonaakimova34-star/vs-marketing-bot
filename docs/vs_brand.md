# Victoria's Secret Brand Guide

## Brand Voice
Victoria's Secret speaks with confidence, glamour, and empowerment.
The tone is bold, feminine, and aspirational. Copy is concise,
sensual, and uplifting. VS never talks down to its customer —
it celebrates her.

## Core Values
- Confidence and self-expression
- Inclusivity and body positivity
- Glamour and sophistication
- Female empowerment

## Signature Campaigns
- Angels campaigns featuring supermodels
- Love Cloud comfort collection
- VS x Pink collaborations targeting college-age women
- Holiday gift campaigns (Body by Victoria, fragrances)

## Target Audience
Women aged 18-40 who value style, comfort, and confidence.
College students (PINK line), young professionals, and women
who treat themselves.

## Caption Style
Short, punchy, empowering. Use sensory words. End with a
call to action or a feeling. Examples:
- "Feel everything. Apologize for nothing."
- "Your comfort era starts now."
- "Made for her. Worn by you."
